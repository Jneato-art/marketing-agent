# Playbook: Organic Social

Use to build an owned audience and brand affinity over time. Compounds, costs time not money, and feeds every other channel.

## Strategy
1. **Pick 1-2 platforms** where your audience actually is — not all of them.
2. **Hook first.** On short-form video the first 1-3 seconds decide everything. Open with tension, a bold claim, or a relatable problem.
3. **Content pillars** (3-4): educate, entertain, prove (results/testimonials), and occasionally sell (~10%).
4. **Consistency > polish.** A sustainable cadence you keep beats a burst you abandon.

## The format that works now (short-form)
Hook (problem/claim) -> quick value or story -> payoff -> soft CTA. Keep it tight. Caption reinforces the hook and adds the CTA.

## Growth tactics
- Reply to every comment for the first hour (signals the algorithm).
- Hop on relevant trends/sounds fast, but bend them to your message.
- Repurpose winners across platforms and into ads.
- Collaborate with adjacent creators to borrow audiences.

## Measurement
Watch retention/watch-time and saves/shares over raw likes — they predict reach. Track follower-to-customer path, not just follower count.

## KPIs
Watch time / retention -> shares & saves -> profile visits -> link clicks -> conversions.

2. Script 5 hook-first short videos.
3. Post daily for 2 weeks and double down on the top performer.
