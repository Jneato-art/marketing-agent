# Playbook: Product Launch

Use when introducing a new product/brand or a major new offer. Goal: a concentrated burst of attention and a spike of early customers you can learn from.

## Pre-launch (2-4 weeks out)
1. Nail positioning (one sentence) and the single launch offer.
2. Build a simple waitlist/landing page with one CTA.
3. Seed an audience: warm list, communities, 5-10 potential partners/creators.
4. Prepare assets: 3 ad concepts, 5 social posts, 1 launch email, 1 demo/teaser video.
5. Line up social proof: even 3-5 early testimonials or a credible founder story.

## Launch week
1. Day 1: announce everywhere at once (email, social, communities, partners).
2. Days 1-3: daily content — story, demo, proof, behind-the-scenes.
3. Add urgency: launch-week pricing or bonus with a real deadline.
4. Reply to every comment/DM — early engagement compounds reach.
5. Activate partners/creators to post in the same window.

## Post-launch (week after)
1. Send a "last chance" email before the offer ends.
2. Capture results and testimonials from first buyers.
3. Retarget visitors who didn't convert.
4. Write the launch retro into the experiments log.

## KPIs
Waitlist size -> launch-day conversion -> week-1 customers -> CAC. 

## Next 3 actions template
1. Draft positioning + offer.
2. Ship the landing page.
3. Line up 5 launch partners.
