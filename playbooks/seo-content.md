# Playbook: SEO & Content

Use for durable, compounding inbound traffic. Slow to start, but it builds an asset that pays for years.

## Strategy
1. **Topic clusters, not random posts.** Pick 3-4 core topics you want to own. Build a pillar page per topic + supporting articles that link to it.
2. **Target intent, not just volume.** Prioritize keywords where the searcher is close to buying ("best X for Y", "X vs Z", "how to [job your product does]").
3. **Match search intent exactly.** Look at what already ranks; give the same format but clearly better and more current.
4. **Win the snippet.** Answer the question in the first 2-3 sentences, then go deep.

## Production loop
- Keyword research -> outline against top results -> draft -> add original value (data, examples, visuals) -> publish -> internally link -> update every 6-12 months.

## Off-page
- Earn links with original data, tools, or strong opinion pieces. Quality > quantity.

## Timeline reality
Expect 3-6 months before meaningful traffic. Front-load "bottom-funnel" pages (comparisons, alternatives, use-cases) for faster revenue impact.

## KPIs
Indexed pages -> rankings for target terms -> organic traffic -> organic-sourced signups/sales.
