# Playbook: Paid Acquisition

Use for fast, controllable, testable growth — when you can afford to pay to learn and your unit economics work.

## Before you spend a dollar
1. Know your numbers: target CAC, average order value, LTV. If you don't know LTV, cap early CAC at <= first-order margin.
2. Have a converting destination (landing page) and tracking in place.
3. Have 3+ creative concepts ready — creative is the #1 lever on modern platforms.

## Test structure
1. Start small: a fixed test budget per concept (e.g. $30-50/day).
2. Test creative angles first, audiences second. Algorithms find the audience; your job is the hook.
3. One variable at a time. Give each test enough budget to reach significance (rule of thumb: ~50 conversions or 3-5 days).
4. Kill anything clearly below baseline. Double down on winners.

## Scaling
- Scale budget on winners ~20-30% at a time (jumps reset learning).
- Refresh creative before fatigue (watch frequency + falling CTR).
- Build retargeting for warm traffic — cheapest conversions you'll get.

## Channel picks
- Meta/TikTok: discovery, DTC, impulse + visual products.
- Google Search: existing high-intent demand.
- YouTube: consideration, demo-heavy products.

## KPIs
CTR -> CPC -> conversion rate -> CAC -> ROAS. North star: CAC vs LTV.
