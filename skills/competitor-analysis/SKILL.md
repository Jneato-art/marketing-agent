# Skill: Competitor Analysis

## When to use
When choosing positioning, pricing, or channels — you need to know what you are up against and where the open lane is.

## Procedure

1. **Pick the set.** 3-6 competitors: a mix of market leaders, direct rivals, and one scrappy challenger.
2. **Profile each** across: positioning/promise, target customer, price, primary channels, content style, social proof, and obvious weakness.
3. **Find their funnel.** Where do they acquire (ads, SEO, social, partnerships)? Look at their site, ad libraries, and top content.
4. **Score the gap.** Identify 2-3 things every competitor does (table stakes) and 1-2 things none do well (your opening).
5. **Fill the matrix.** Use `templates/competitor-matrix.md`.
6. **Save** to `knowledge/brands/<brand>/research.md` (append) and note the winning gap in the index.

## Where to look
- Competitor website + pricing page
- Meta Ad Library / TikTok Creative Center (what ads they run)
- Their top-ranking blog posts (SEO strategy)
- Reviews (what customers love and hate — your wedge)

## Output
A filled `competitor-matrix.md` plus a 3-sentence "how we win" statement: the gap, why it's defensible, and the first move to claim it.

## Self-learning
Record each competitor's strongest move and weakest point so the next analysis starts from this baseline rather than a blank page.
