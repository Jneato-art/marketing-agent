# Skill: Content Calendar

## When to use
When a channel in the plan needs consistent publishing (social, blog, email, YouTube).

## Procedure

1. **Set cadence** per channel based on what's sustainable (e.g. 3 TikToks/wk, 1 blog/wk, 2 emails/wk). Consistency beats volume.
2. **Define content pillars.** 3-4 recurring themes tied to audience pains/desires (educate, entertain, prove, sell). Aim for ~60% value, ~30% story, ~10% direct sell.
3. **Generate hooks.** For each slot, write a specific hook/angle, not just a topic. The hook is 80% of performance.
4. **Assign format & CTA.** What format (short video, carousel, listicle), and what action each piece drives.
5. **Schedule** into `templates/content-calendar.csv` with columns: date, channel, pillar, hook, format, CTA, status, owner.
6. **Batch the work.** Group similar pieces so production is efficient.

## Output
A 30-day filled `content-calendar.csv` plus the first week's content drafted (delegate drafting to the `copywriter` agent).

## Self-learning
After publishing, record which hooks/pillars over-performed in the experiments log; weight future calendars toward winners.
